To schedule a new ride:

Visit Home page, click "Add Ride" in the nevigation bar and fill out the requested info. If confirmed, the ride will appear on your homepage calendar.


To edit or delete a ride:

Visit Home page, click "Edit Ride" in the nevigation and fill out the requested info. If edited, the updated ride will appear on your homepage calendar. If deleted, the ride will no longer appear on your homepage calendar.


For any other questions or concerns:

Visit Cal Poly's DRC Website or visit Building 124.
