To schedule a new ride:

Visit Scheduling New Ride and fill out the requested info for the student. Confirm with student that the ride appears on their homepage calendar.


To edit or delete a ride:

Visit Edit/Delete Ride and fill out the requested info for the student. If edited, the updated ride will appear on student's homepage calendar. If deleted, the ride will no longer appear on student's homepage calendar.
